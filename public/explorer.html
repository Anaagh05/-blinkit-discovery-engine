Discovery Engine

Analyzing thousands of user reviews with Google Gemini to decode why users stick to their habits and how to unlock new product discovery in quick commerce.

How Data Is Gathered

Our multi-source scraping pipeline pulls user feedback from 6 platforms for maximum coverage and bias reduction.

—

Google Play Store

Star ratings + text reviews. The largest single corpus, skewing toward frequent users.

—

Apple App Store

iOS-specific feedback. Often more detailed and sentiment-rich than Play Store.

—

Reddit

Community discussions. More nuanced opinions and edge-case frustrations surface here.

—

Facebook

Public page comments and post reactions. Captures a broader demographic slice.

—

Instagram

Comment threads on Blinkit posts. Reveals brand perception and discovery triggers.

—

Others (Quora, Blogs, X)

Long-form opinions and niche platforms for cross-validation of major themes.

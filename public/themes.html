Discovery Engine

Analyzing thousands of user reviews with Google Gemini to decode why users stick to their habits and how to unlock new product discovery in quick commerce.

How Themes Are Identified

Our theme extraction process uses a holistic LLM approach — not keyword matching — to find deep behavioral patterns.

Theme Extraction Pipeline

  1. Full-corpus ingestion: All 2,313 sentiment-tagged reviews are loaded into Gemini 2.5 Pro's extended context window in a single prompt.
  2. Holistic clustering: The model identifies recurring behavioral patterns across all sources simultaneously — not per-review, but as emergent clusters.
  3. Question mapping: Each extracted theme is mapped to the 8 research questions it helps answer (shown below each theme).
  4. Cross-source validation: Only themes that appear across ≥2 independent platforms with 92%+ agreement are retained.
